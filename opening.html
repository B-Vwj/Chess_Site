Opening Phase in Chess

In Chess, there are three phases, the Opening, Middlegame, and Endgame.
With the opening phase, you and your opponent can dictate how the game is played within just a few moves of the game! We can often distinguish games simply enough my either a quiet game, or a more aggressive game with lots of action.
Here are some just a few openings that I play:

  • Alekhine's Defense
  • Italian
